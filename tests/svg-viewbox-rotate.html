SVG viewbox rotation

This demonstrates what it takes to achieve rotating the svg. Note that the red lines are originally horizontal. Pay attention to the following:
  • rotate(90,CX,CY)
  • translate(CY-CX, CX-CY)
  • there's width on the wrapper, which is post-rotation stretched via viewbox="0,0,H,W", preserveAspectRatio=none
  • viewbox="0,0,H,W" instead of "0,0,W,H"
